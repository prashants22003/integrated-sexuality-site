My wish for you

You or your body is not the problem.

It is time to remember: your body has its own beautiful story.

Deep within your pelvis lives a well of power, pleasure, and life. It has always been there, waiting for you to listen.
You are not broken. Even in pain, numbness, or doubt: You are whole.

Your body may be holding tension from stress, childbirth, medical experiences, emotional strain, or moments when you didn't feel safe. Not as a flaw, but as protection.

Healing begins not by forcing change, but by creating safety. When the nervous system feels supported, the body can soften, release, and reconnect to sensation, trust, and aliveness.

What is Integrated Sexuality?

A body-led approach to healing, intimacy, and sexual wellbeing

Integrated Sexuality combines pelvic care, somatic sexology, nervous system regulation, and relational bodywork. This work supports the whole person: Body, emotions, history, and relationships.

We do not focus on performance, technique, or quick fixes. We begin with safety. When the nervous system is supported, the body naturally releases held tension, restores tissue health, and reawakens sensation.

Your pelvis is the centre of instinct, protection, pleasure, and vitality. Experiences like stress, trauma, birth, surgery, or relational strain can create patterns of holding. This work gently unwinds those patterns from the inside out.

You might be here because:

  • Sex is painful or numb
  • You feel disconnected from desire or sensation
  • Your body changed after birth, menopause, or medical events
  • You understand your patterns but can't shift them
  • You want change that lives in the body, not just the mind

You are not broken. These skills can be learned.

About Me

Hi, I'm Anchal Kaushal

A certified Somatic Sexologist and Holistic Pelvic Care Therapist.

I work with high-capacity women, men, and couples who are successful in many areas of life — yet feel stuck, disconnected, or frustrated when it comes to intimacy, pelvic health, or pleasure.

My Mission

To raise the standard of sexual wellness and pelvic care through trauma-informed, whole-person work that addresses the nervous system — not just the symptoms.

My Approach

Often, we try to change the mind first and hope the body follows. I work the other way around. Your nervous system shapes how you experience trust, pleasure, connection, and confidence. Through breath, touch, movement, and presence, I help safety, pleasure, and power become something you feel, not just something you think about.
