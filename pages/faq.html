Everything you need to know about the practice, the sessions, and what to expect on your journey.

Somatic Sexology Sessions focus on emotional healing, embodiment, and your relationship with sexuality, pleasure, and your nervous system.

Holistic Pelvic Care Treatments are hands-on therapeutic bodywork that restore the function, sensitivity, and integrity of your pelvic floor muscles, tissues, and nerves.

Couples Intimacy Training is a guided somatic learning process that helps couples deepen trust, consent, communication, and intimacy.

In simple terms:

  • → Sessions: emotional and somatic healing
  • → Treatments: physical pelvic rehabilitation
  • → Training: somatic intimacy education for couples

Sessions may include:

  • Somatic awareness and embodiment practices
  • Breathwork and nervous system regulation
  • Emotional integration
  • Education about pleasure and anatomy
  • Optional hands-on bodywork (in person, when appropriate)

These sessions help you reconnect with your body, release shame or numbness, and deepen your capacity for pleasure and intimacy.

* Available online and in person.

Holistic Pelvic Care is therapeutic pelvic bodywork that restores function, circulation, and sensitivity in the pelvic floor. It can help with:

Urinary incontinence
Pelvic organ prolapse
Painful menstruation
Endometriosis
Pelvic pain or tightness
Pain during intimacy
Genital numbness
Scar tissue and adhesions
Postpartum recovery
Erection inconsistencies and ejaculatory timing
Prostate Health

Treatments last 2.5–3 hours and work directly on both tissues and the nervous system.

Yes. Internal pelvic work is an essential and integral part of Holistic Pelvic Care.

This allows direct treatment of the pelvic floor muscles and fascia to restore function, circulation, and sensitivity.

All work is trauma-informed, gentle, and conducted with clear consent. You remain in control throughout the process.

De-Armouring is a somatic bodywork process that helps release chronic tension and protective holding patterns stored in the body due to stress, trauma, or emotional suppression.

A session may include:

  • Guided breathwork
  • Gentle, sustained therapeutic touch to areas of tension
  • Nervous system regulation practices
  • Sound, movement, and emotional release
  • Integration and grounding

This process helps restore sensitivity, emotional flow, and a sense of safety and openness in the body. Each session is guided at your pace and nervous system readiness.

Couples Intimacy Training helps partners rebuild trust, connection, and intimacy. This includes:

  • Consent education
  • Communication tools
  • Conscious touch practices
  • Somatic embodiment tools
  • Nervous system regulation practices

This training helps couples deepen emotional and physical connection in a safe and supported way.
